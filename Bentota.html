Welcome to Bentota Turtle Hatchery!

At the Bentota Turtle Hatchery, where our devoted crew works relentlessly to safeguard and conserve these unique species, you may learn about the wonders of sea turtles. Our hatchery is a sanctuary for sea turtles, offering a secure haven for their nesting, hatching, and rehabilitation. It is situated in the gorgeous seaside town of Bentota.

You may contribute directly to the preservation and conservation of these extinct animals by going to the Bentota Turtle Hatchery. Join us in our dedication to preserving sea turtles and their natural environments.

NOTE:Please take note that in order to protect the welfare and safety of sea turtles, the Bentota Turtle Hatchery rigorously adheres to moral standards. We politely ask that visitors follow these rules while they are there.

Plan your trip now and visit the Bentota Turtle Hatchery on a voyage of understanding, compassion, and conservation. We can improve sea turtles' lives by working together.
