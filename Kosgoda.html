Welcome to Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery!

Nestled along Sri Lanka's stunning coastline, the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery offers marine turtles some hope. Being a specialized conservation center, we are dedicated to safeguarding the survival of these wonderful species in spite of several challenges.

We work very hard at the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery to rescue, rehab, and teach sea turtles in order to protect and conserve them. Our hatchery provides a safe haven where these ancient seafarers can nest, hatch, and eventually re-enter the ocean.

NOTE: Please be aware that the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery adheres to moral standards to guarantee the welfare and security of sea turtles. During their stay, guests are respectfully asked to follow these rules.
