About Us

We are committed to the preservation and protection of sea turtles in order to ensure their continued existence. Sea turtles find refuge at our hatchery, where we rescue, treat, and release them into their natural habitats. We work hard to have a beneficial effect on sea turtle survival and welfare by being strongly committed to environmental stewardship, education, and community involvement.

Let's work together to make sure sea turtles are alive and well for future generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect sea turtles by:
  • Rescue and rehabilitation: Giving wounded or ill sea turtles a safe haven and providing them with specialized care and medical attention to support their recovery and flourish.
  • Nest Protection & Conservation: safeguarding sea turtle nests on our neighborhood beaches, putting precautions in place to protect them from danger, and keeping an eye on them up until the hatchlings emerge.
  • Raising public awareness and educating the public about the value of protecting sea turtles via educational initiatives, neighborhood engagement projects, and engaging experiences for visitors of all ages.
  • Research and Collaboration: We are actively partnering with national and international groups to advance conservation efforts while doing scientific research to further our understanding of sea turtles, their habitats, and the difficulties they confront.
  • Sustainable Operations: We advocate for responsible tourism that protects and promotes the environment while promoting sustainable operations within our hatchery operations.
Ways to contribute:
  • Stop by: Visit our hatchery and see the amazing job we perform for yourself. Discover the problems that sea turtles encounter and the stages of their lives. To strengthen your bond with these incredible creatures, take part in hatchling releases and educational events.
  • Volunteer: Volunteer with us to help our conservation efforts by giving of your time and abilities. Your participation may have a big impact, whether it's helping to care for turtles or participating in beach clean-ups and community engagement.
  • Donate: Our rescue, rehabilitation, research, and instructional endeavors cannot be sustained without your financial assistance. The survival and well-being of sea turtles are directly benefited by your donation.

Join Us:

Join us in our effort to safeguard sea turtles and the environments in which they live. You can make a real impact in the lives of these amazing animals by visiting our hatchery, taking part in educational events, volunteering, or making a gift.
