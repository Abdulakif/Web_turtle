Welcome to Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery!

Sri Lanka's Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a sanctuary devoted to the preservation and defense of sea turtles. The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery, a conservation program devoted to saving sea turtles, is situated on Sri Lanka's stunning southern coast. Our hatchery serves as a haven for these amazing creatures, offering a secure environment in which they can hatch, develop, and flourish.

We at the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery are committed to advancing sustainable lifestyles and raising public awareness of the value of sea turtle protection in order to ensure sea turtle survival for upcoming generations.

It's interesting and different to see the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery. At the hatchery, you may engage in the following activities:
  1. Discover intriguing information about the many kinds of sea turtles, their life cycles, their nesting behaviors, and the conservation initiatives being implemented to protect them. The majority of the time, knowledgeable staff members are on hand to answer your inquiries and give information.
  2. See the breathtaking sight of hatchlings being released into the ocean. You might be able to take part in or watch the release of newborn turtles, helping to ensure their survival and population expansion, depending on the time of year and your availability.
  3. Enjoy the excitement of feeding marine turtles. You could get the chance to feed these wonderful creatures and see them feed up close while being supervised by hatchery professionals.
  4. Visit a Rehabilitation Facility: A few turtle hatcheries also serve as rescue and rehabilitation facilities for ill or damaged turtles. See how these turtles are treated and cared for before being released back into the wild by taking a tour of the rehabilitation centers.
  5. Volunteer Opportunities: Check out the hatchery's volunteer possibilities if you have a strong passion for conservation and want to have a more direct impact. You could get the chance to help with tasks like beach cleanups, nest guarding, or hatchling care.
  6. Investigate the Hatchery's Facilities: Take a tour of the hatchery and look at its features. See the many sea turtle development phases, nesting cages, and nesting locations. Learn more about the hatchery's function in the preservation and protection of sea turtles.
  7. Visit the hatchery's gift shop to find a selection of items with sea turtle themes, such as crafts, artwork, clothes, and instructional materials. Supporting the hatchery's conservation efforts by purchasing mementos from the gift store.
  8. Consider contributing to the hatchery to support conservation. Your donation will immediately aid their activities for sea turtle rescue, rehabilitation, and conservation. The hatchery's continued operations and the welfare of sea turtles depend heavily on donations.
To guarantee the safety and welfare of the turtles, keep in mind to adhere to any instructions supplied by the hatchery. Enjoy your trip and savor the chance to interact with these amazing animals and the committed people who are fighting to protect them.
